About Me

Hey there! I'm a front-end web developer who loves to learn and solve problems.

Developing websites was something that I became interested in during college. I'm someone who has a powerful desire to learn and who loves to dive headfirst into a new subject or a new technology. I enjoy that magic feeling of getting something to work for the first time, especially if I had to solve a lot of problems to make it happen. I feel that web development really compliments my strengths and it's something that I'm genuinely really passionate about!

I have two sides: one that is creative and artistic, and another that is logical and curious. First I want to know how things work, then I want to make them look good doing it.
